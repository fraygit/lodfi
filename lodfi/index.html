Our Mission

By the norm of the Gospel truth and values, Little Ones Development Foundation, Inc. envisions its graduates as effective instruments and channels of God's love for man -- able to live and translate academic excellence into a life of dignity for himself, for the community, for the country and for God.

It is therefore its mission to provide and promote an integrative quality education for the wholesome development of every child whose values will lead him/her to constantly quest for what is truly good, pleaseing and perfect --- making him free, loving, industrious, responsible, and productive contributor to the betterment and welfare of his country and fellowmen.
